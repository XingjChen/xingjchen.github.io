About Me

Xingjian Chen | 陈行健

I am currently a second-year Computer Science Ph.D. candidate in East Asian Bioinformatics and Computational Biology Lab at City University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Ka-Chun Wong. I am interested in Applied Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Bioinformatics, and Data Science. My current research focuses on deep learning algorithm development in the field of Bioinformatics. Besides, I am also interested in deep learning applications in Medical Imaging and Biomedical Text Mining.

Before arriving at CityU, I received my Master Degree at College of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing Agricultural University under the supervision of Prof. Wei Xue.
